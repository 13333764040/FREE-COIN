ECOSYSTEM
   

 SHIBSC is a decentralized, community driven, fair launched Meme Token in BSC. It is a decentralized community experiment. 67% of the tokens has been burned to the BLACKHOLE address. Everyone has to participate on the fair open market, meaning devs have no more ownership of any tokens at all. The first place to buy or sell SHIBSC is PancakeSwap.


   

Tokenomics

6% fee per transaction. 3% for all Shiba BSC($SHIBSC) holders, 3% of every transaction is locked as liquidity in the PancakeSwap SHIBSC/BNB pool, creating an ever rising price floor.


   

RFI Static Rewards

Holders earn passive rewards through static reflection as they watch their balance of Shiba BSC($SHIBSC) grow indefinitely.
